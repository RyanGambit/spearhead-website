About Spearhead

We share a common mission: help business owners navigate their most important transaction, and help acquirers find the right partners.

How we started

Spearhead was born from FirePower Capital's deal origination engine and spun out as an independent entity in September 2022.

We saw that the earliest stages of deal-making were consistently under-invested in by acquirers. The best opportunities were being missed, not because of bad strategy, but because of limited capacity and outdated outreach.

Today, Spearhead sits at the intersection of M&A advisory, revenue operations, and data science. We combine real relationships with smart systems to help our clients find and close the right deals.
