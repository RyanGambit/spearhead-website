The CDaaS process

We operate as an extension of your team to generate meaningful, primarily proprietary deal flow.

Phase 1
Target research
We leverage our market mapping tools and networks to continuously identify, curate, and prioritize targets throughout the mandate.
1
Targets are shared with you in "research waves" for review and feedback.
2
You approve or disqualify targets. No outreach is made to disqualified companies.
3
This identification process is ongoing and adapts as new insights emerge.
Key deliverable: Curated, pre-outreach target lists delivered in waves throughout the mandate.
Phase 2
Pre-outreach preparation
Before outreach begins, we align on your positioning and prepare all necessary materials for a successful mandate.
1
We train the team on your narrative, acquisition criteria, and value proposition.
2
Develop outreach strategy and collateral, tailored to your brand and objectives.
3
We train the team on any relevant supporting documents and collateral.
Key deliverable: Outreach collateral and guidelines.
Phase 3
Outreach and advisory
We manage multi-channel outreach and act as an advisor from first contact to handoff, continuously adapting to your shifting priorities.
1
Execute thoughtful, high-frequency outreach across multiple channels to engage and qualify targets.
2
Share detailed deal memos for interested, qualified targets and facilitate introductions at your discretion.
3
Support the deal beyond the intro, maintaining warm relationships and keeping momentum.
Key deliverables: Deal memos, client-target intros, and ongoing support through closing.
